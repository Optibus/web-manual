Generating a Weekly Schedule

Clicking on any day, will show the Gantt for that day. We will use the Homogeneous schedule from the first part of the Block Homogeneity example.

Open up the Optibize preference button and switch on Multi Day:

Running optimization produces an optimized weekly schedule with optimization between days. A multi day optimization typically takes longer to run than the single day optimizations that we have seen so far.

A quick examination of the Vehicles Gantts for each day of the week, shows that they are each sign-homogeneous.
