Filtering the Gantt Views

The two Gantts show an immense amount of information. You can use the Filter button to limit the view. Suppose for example, that we want to see blocks for sign 150. On the Vehicles Gantt, open the Filter:

In the Trips Sign field, click over the Search for Signs area. You are offered a list of available signs:

You may choose as many as you like (use the scroll bar to see more). We will choose sign 150 and click Apply. Here is the result:

The filter extracted all blocks including sign 150.

The choice of filters for the Drivers Gantt is much more extensive, and we won't show the details here. However, if you again filter on sign 150, here is the result:

The filter extracted all drivers (duties) including sign 150 during the working day.
