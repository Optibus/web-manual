Continuous Attendance

Preference Overview

This preference sets the maximum time a vehicle may be left unattended. The maximum unattended time has, for example implications for driver changeover delays.

Allowance is made for a stops where a vehicle may be left unattended - for example inside a depot.

There are two preference templates available only one of which may be used and then only as a single instance.
