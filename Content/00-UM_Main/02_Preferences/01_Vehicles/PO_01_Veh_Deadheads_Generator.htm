Deadheads Generator

Preference Overview

The Deadheads Generator preference is an internal device to calculate and complete deadheads missing from the Deadhead Catalog. Selecting this preference enables it.

  • The deadheads calculation algorithm for the Generator can be supplied by the customer
  • Deadhead, pull out or pull in icons in a Gantt having a white dot in the center, for example like this, are auto-generated by the system based on this preference

The Deadheads Generator may b e selected either by using Add Preference or Load Template. The one available template, Deadhead generator - default, only has expert Mode.
