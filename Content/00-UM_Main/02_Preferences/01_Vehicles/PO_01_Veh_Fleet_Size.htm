Fleet Size

Preference Overview

The vehicle fleet size may be a single number or specified by vehicle type. It will typically be larger than the minimum number of vehicles specified in an optimized schedule. The Operator might want to force a larger number of vehicles into day to day operations as a vehicle load balancing device. He may wish to ensure for example, that

  • Particular vehicles are always in use
  • Particular vehicles are never permanently parked in a depot (never used)
  • More of his fleet will be in use than the minimum
