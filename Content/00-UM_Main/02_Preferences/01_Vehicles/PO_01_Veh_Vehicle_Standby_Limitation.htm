Vehicle Standby Limitation

Work-flow to Use Vehicle Standby Limitation

The Vehicle Standby Limitation preference is accessed through Load Template and only one instance of it is permitted.

Preference Overview

The Vehicle Standby Limitation preference sets a maximum continuous vehicle standby time range for which vehicles may not be in use. It is a vehicle load balancing device.

Templates Available from

Vehicle standby limitation

Purpose:

Sets the maximum continuous vehicle standby time range for which vehicles may not be in use.

The maximum standby time is set as a range. Standby time up to the low end of the range carries no penalty. Standby time between the low end and the high end carries a sliding penalty. The high end of the range is set as a constraint by default.

Prerequisites: None.

Opening Dialog:

Points to note:

  • Vehicle standby time range: The Vehicle standby time range is the time range for maximum continuous standby.
  • Constraint: The upper limit of the standby time range can be made a constraint by setting Constraint to Yes. This is the default setting.
  • Penalty: The penalty is imposed when the standby time is between the two limits on a sliding scale from zero at the low end to the full penalty at the high end
  • Only one instance of this template may be used
