Boarding Time Relaxation

Work-flow to Use Boarding Time Relaxation

  • There is currently one template
  • Use Load Template to set up the preference
  • Repeat Load Template for additional instances of the template

Preference Overview

A provision for boarding time may be made in the customer schedule data. Here we allow for the possibility of reducing it at the expense of a sliding penalty that increases with the reduction of a scheduled trip boarding time. If boarding time is defined, it will appear in the relevant vehicle information box:

In the example, the boarding time extends the scheduled trip time by 10 min.

Templates Available from

Boarding Time Relaxation

Purpose:

Sets the penalty for relaxing the boarding time.

Prerequisites: The boarding time must be defined in the Dataset for the schedule.

Opening Dialog:

Boarding Time Relaxation preference

Points to note:

The only entry field here is the opening penalty. It increases nearly linearly with boarding time violation.
