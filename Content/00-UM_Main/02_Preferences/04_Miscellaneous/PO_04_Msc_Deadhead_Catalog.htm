Deadhead Catalog

Preference Overview

This preference enables you to add to the loaded schedule a previously imported deadhead catalog. The preference is accessed using Add Preference. There are no templates.

Add Preference

Prerequisites: You should have one or more imported deadhead catalogs.

Opening Dialog:

Points to note:

  • Catalog: Clicking the Catalog field opens a list of available catalogs:
  • Alternatively, you can enter the name of a catalog by hand. If it does not exist you will be told:
  • You may also choose Default, to choose the default catalog built into the Dataset, if any
  • Your chosen catalog will be used at the next and subsequent optimizations

In the absence of a Deadhead Catalog, you can use the Deadheads Generator to generate deadheads.
