Taxi Schedule

Work-flow to Use Taxi Schedule

  • The Taxi Schedule preference is accessed through Load Template
  • There is only one template available, and only one instance of it is permitted

Preference Overview

Driver shuttles may operated by the drivers themselves as part of a duty (instead of a third party). This preference sets driver shuttle (taxi) schedule strategy.

Templates Available from

Driver Duty Work Time Preference

Purpose:

As stated in the Overview.

Prerequisites: None.

Opening Dialog:

Points to note:

  • Pair grouping strategy: When set to Yes, shuttles are driven by drivers as part of their duty and there is no need for specific shuttle scheduling (duties or drivers)
  • Group with depot trips: When set to Yes, shuttle trips a re grouped with depot pull out/pull in trips.

    • You cannot use Group with depot trips with Pair grouping strategy set to Yes.

  • Max passengers: This is the maximum number of passengers in a shuttle trip, including the driver
  • Max wait time: This is the maximum time that a driver may wait for a shuttle before or after his duty.
