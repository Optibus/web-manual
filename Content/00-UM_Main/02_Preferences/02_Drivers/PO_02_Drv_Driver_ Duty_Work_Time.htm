Driver Duty Work Time

Work-flow to Use Driver Duty Work Time

  • The Driver Duty Work Time preference is accessed through Load Template
  • There is only one template available, and only one instance of it is permitted

Preference Overview

This preference guides the choice of driver work time by ranking work times with penalties. It does this by setting a sliding penalty for driver duty work time by time interval. See for example, Figure 1-1 below.

The table in Figure 1-1 is customer specific.

The penalties as shown work like this: The base penalty is 50. Between 00:00 and 04:00, the penalty is 50 * 1.0 = 50.

From 04:00 to 07:00, the penalty starts at 50 * 1.0 and drops proportionally to 50 * 0.5 = 25.

From 07:00 to 08:00, the penalty starts at 50 * 0.5 and drops proportionally to 50 * 0.2 = 10.

From 08:00 to 09:00, the penalty starts at 50 * 0.2 and drops proportionally to 50 * 0.0 = 0.

From 09:00 to 10:00, the penalty remains at 0.

From 10:00 to 12:00, the penalty starts at 50 * 0.0 and rises proportionally to 50 * 0.3 = 15.

From 12:00 onwards, he penalty is fixed at 50 * 0.3

For this example, the preferred duty times would be 9 to 10 hours followed by 8 to 9 hours.

The decimal numbers that you enter for each time range are multipliers for the base penalty per hour within the range.
