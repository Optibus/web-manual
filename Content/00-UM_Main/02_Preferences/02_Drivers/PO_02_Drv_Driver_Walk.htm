Driver Walk

Work-flow to Use Driver Walk

The Driver Walk preference is accessed through Load Template and only one instance of it is permitted.

Preference Overview

A driver may move on foot for a vehicle changeover between very close locations after a break or a split break. Maximum walking distance and walking time are specified here.

Setting a high penalty reduces the number of walks.

Templates Available from

Driver Walk

Purpose:

As stated in the Overview

Prerequisites: None.

Opening Dialog:

Point to note:

  • Penalty: The higher the penalty, the less the number of walks. You can leave the penalty at zero to prefer unlimited walks within the time and distance limits
  • Max walking distance and Walking time: Ensure that these two values are realistic and mutually consistent. The default shown of up to 200 m in 1 minute may be a bit demanding!
