Driver Standby

Work-flow to Use Driver Standby

The Driver Standby preference is accessed through Load Template and only one instance of it is permitted.

Preference Overview

Driver standbys are unavoidable. The preference sets a minimum break time that can be considered a standby. Two standby penalties are imposed: A fixed penalty per standby and an increasing penalty for every minute a standby exceed the minimum break time.

Templates Available from

Driver Standby

Purpose:

As stated in the Overview

Prerequisites: None.

Opening Dialog:

Point to note:

  • Minimum break: This is the minimum break that can incur a standby penalty
  • Constant Penalty: A fixed penalty incurred for each break over the minimum
  • Break time unit penalty: The penalty incurred for each minute of break time over the minimum
