Topic Section Grouping

The Preferences are organized into four topic groups, Vehicles, Drivers, Depots and Miscellaneous. The fourth group is a "catchall" to cover items that do not fit into the first three groups. Look at the preferences opening window, each of the five topics shown is a toggle. For example, click Vehicles:

These are the Vehicle topics in use for the current open schedule. This presentation is ideal for schedule maintenance based on modifying pre-installed or your own existing preferences.

To see all of the available Vehicle topics, click the All button:

Topics in use are checked. The topic list runs of the screen and you will need the scroll bar to see them all.
