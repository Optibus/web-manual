Preferences Window Tool Bar

Save and Cancel

The Save and Cancel buttons appear below Preference dialog in the system. The Save button saves all of your preferences to the current open schedule. Your changes will take effect after the next optimization. Remember, Save will commit all of your changes since entering Preferences or since your last Save - whichever later.

The Cancel button discards any pending preference additions or changes you made since opening the Preferences window.
