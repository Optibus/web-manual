Adding a Preference by Loading a Template

The Quick Tour case, Adding Pre Trip and Post trip Elements will be used for illustrative purposes.

Open the Preferences window and open Pre Post Trip preference under the Vehicles topic.

Click the Load Template button:

Load Template dialog

The left-most tab, Mine is a private user area for template storage. The Icon tab is a customer named and different for each customer. Templates entered here will be available to all template users. The tab is a receptacle for -prepared Templates designed to meet common requirements for the open preference topic. Here is what we have for Pre Post Trip:

Save as Template

Save as template works as follows. Clicking it opens a standard Save As dialog:

Enter a name and an optional description and then Save. It does nothing to the open Schedule. Templates are available to any customer-authorized user. The template will appear under the "Mine" and customer tabs in Figure 1-1.
