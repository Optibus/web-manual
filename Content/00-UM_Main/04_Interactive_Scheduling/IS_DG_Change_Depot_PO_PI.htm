Changing a Depot for Pull out/Pull in

You can change a depot pull in destination or depot pull out origin at the end or beginning of a duty or at he beginning or ending of split break. We use a depot pull out as an example:

▶  To change the origin of a depot pull out:

1. Open the information box for the pull out to be changed.

2. Click Edit. The Edit information box is displayed.

3. Click the button.

The pull out is removed from the Drivers Gantt.

4. Mouse-over the pre trip or the first trip of duty 211:

Notice the small blue plus sign before the duty.

5. Click the plus sign. The following dialog opens:

6. Click the Type field. A drop down list of available types opens:

7. Choose Deadhead.

8. Click the From: field. A drop list of available stops is displayed:

9. Choose (say) COC Campus.

10. Click Add. The pull out is added

11. Click Assign Vehicles to commit your changes.

The same method is applicable to pull ins, pull ins before a split break and pull outs after a split break.
