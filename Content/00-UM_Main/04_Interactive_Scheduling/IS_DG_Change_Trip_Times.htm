Changing trip times

To illustrate the procedure we will use duty 201 from Figure 1-1. The first duty for the day is a trip on sign 196. Here is its information box:

Suppose that it is found that on Sunday morning this trip has a delay of 7 minutes. We will edit this trip to reflect the situation.

To change the trip times:

Click Edit in the information box:

Information box ready for editing

All of the time fields are open for editing. In addition, the left hand time field (red circle) allows us to add boarding time to the trip by entering an earlier time than the shown trip start time.

By default it sets itself to the start time of 05:00. Enter 04:53.

Also extend the trip length by 5 min by increasing the shown length of 59 min to 64 min:

We could have changed the trip end time to 06:04 and the trip duration would have been updated.

Click Save.

In Figure 1-4 below, observe that in addition to the change-bar, the changed trip element has a black dot indicating that it has been manually changed. Notice also the red exclamation mark at the beginning of the row. It indicates a duty issue that can be found from the bottom status line. Our edit has caused a warning to be issued as shown by the Duty Issues warning:
