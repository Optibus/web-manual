Replacing a Taxi with a Deadhead

It may be possible to replace a taxi with a deadhead:

  1. A pull out at the beginning of a duty
  2. A pull in at the end of a duty
  3. A pull in at the beginning of a split break
  4. A pull out at the end of a split break
