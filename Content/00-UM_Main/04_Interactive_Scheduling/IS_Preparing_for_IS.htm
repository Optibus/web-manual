Preparing for an Interactive Scheduling Session

At the top of the Gantt display, you will have noticed that there is an option to save or discard your changes, for example after setting preferences or running an optimization:

The same applies to manual editing. Until you feel comfortable using manual editing, we suggest that you start by backing up your existing schedule using Save As from the Context Menu.

To enter Manual Editing mode, click the Manual Edit button in the top toolbar. An editing area (in the red box) called the stack, opens above the regular Gantt:
