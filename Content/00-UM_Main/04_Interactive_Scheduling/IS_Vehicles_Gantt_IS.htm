Vehicles Gantt Interactive Editing

What can be edited

  • Trips can start and end times may be edited
  • Trips may be removed
  • Vehicle block information boxes offer editable fields: Vehicle ID and vehicle type

Changing trip times

The method is the same as for changing Driver trip times. See Drivers Gantt: Changing trip times.

Deleting a trip

Deleting a trip is the same as for deleting a Driver trip. See Drivers Gantt: Deleting a Trip.
